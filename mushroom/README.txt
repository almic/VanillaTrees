mush1 & mush2 are brown mushrooms.
mush3 & mush4 are red mushrooms.

mush1 & mush3 are the "normal" variants of the huge mushroom, ranging from 5 to 7 blocks tall.
mush2 & mush4 are the "large" variants of the huge mushroom, either being 9, 11, or 13 blocks high.

In the wild, the "large" variants of the huge mushroom occur at 5% chance, with 95% of huge mushrooms being the "normal" 5-7 blocks high.
